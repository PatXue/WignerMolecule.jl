\section{Ferromagnet}
For the ferromagnet, temperature and energy are in units of meV, and order
parameters are the spin expectation (magnetization) and $\eta_z$ expectation.
\subsection{Zero Field}
\begin{itemize}
    \item State is ordered below $T=4.5$ meV ($\approx 52$ K), when initialized
    in ground state (high bias field), also see heat capacity spike at that temperature
    \item Transition temperature does not seem affected by system size
\end{itemize}
\begin{center}
    \includegraphics[width=0.75\textwidth]{plots/fm_mag.png}\\
    \includegraphics[width=0.75\textwidth]{plots/fm_heatcap.png}
\end{center}

\subsection{Finite Bias Field}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Applying a constant bias field on the spins
    \item Swept temperature below, at, and above transition (4.5, 5.5, 6.5 meV)
    \item Varied system size between 20$\times$20, 40$\times$40, and 80$\times$80
    \item At all system sizes, below transition temperature, $\eta_z$ is flat for
    low fields
\end{itemize}

\end{center}

\section{Antiferromagnet-Ferroelectric}
For AFM-FE, the Hamiltonian is normalized, and the energy and T are in units
of the Hamiltonian (32.98 meV). The order parameters are the expectation of
norm of in-plane $\vec{\eta}$ and the Fourier spin correlation at M.
\subsection{Zero Field}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Spins don't show a clear transition, but in plane $\eta_{xy}$ does show a transition
    at $T=0.3\approx 114$ K
    \item Also see a peak in heat capacity at the same temperature
\end{itemize}
\begin{center}
    \includegraphics[width=0.9\textwidth]{plots/afm_fe_mag.png}
    \includegraphics[width=0.9\textwidth]{plots/afm_fe_energy.png}
\end{center}

\subsection{Finite Field}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Alternating bias field on spins (wave vector M)
    \item With bias field, system seems to transition to phase at some finite bias field, doesn’t change with system size
    \item Increasing temperature moves transition to higher field
    \item Eta-only runs show strong ordering up to very high temperatures
\end{itemize}
\begin{center}
    \includegraphics[width=0.9\textwidth]{plots/afm_fe_bias_mag.png}
\end{center}

\section{Stripe}
For stripe, the Hamiltonian is normalized, and the energy and T are in units
of the Hamiltonian (37.56 meV). The order parameters are the Fourier correlations
of in-plane $\vec{\eta}$ at M, and spins at M/2
\subsection{Zero Field}
\begin{itemize}
    \item See transition in spins, $\eta$, and heat capacity at T=0.5 ($\approx 436$ K)
\end{itemize}
\begin{center}
    \includegraphics[width=0.9\textwidth]{plots/stripe_mag.png}
    \includegraphics[width=0.9\textwidth]{plots/stripe_sus.png}
\end{center}
\subsection{Finite Field}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Bias field is in stripe pattern on the spins
    \item See significant flat region in correlation vs. B at high T which seems to grow with T
    \item Seems to line up with skyrmion susceptibility falling
\end{itemize}
